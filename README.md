## Features
* **App is capable of :** 
	 * IT can play Audio
	 * It can play Video
	 * Volume up & down functionality
	 * Slider to show progress of Song
	 * option to select song on click
	 * play & pause Button
	 * auto stop song on back or application closing 
	 * simple and nice UI
